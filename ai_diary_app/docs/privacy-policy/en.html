ArtDiary AI Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Important Notice: ArtDiary AI prioritizes the protection of user privacy. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully before using our services.

1. Personal Information Collection Items and Methods

1.1 Information We Collect

ArtDiary AI collects the following information:

A. Information Provided Directly by Users

  • Required: Email address (for login and account management)
  • Optional: Profile photo, user-uploaded photos

B. Information Automatically Collected During Service Use

  • Diary Content: Text and titles of diary entries created by users
  • AI-Generated Images: Images generated through Gemini API
  • Emotion Analysis Data: Emotion keywords and statistics analyzed by AI
  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, app version, language settings
  • Usage Statistics: App launch count, diary writing frequency, feature usage patterns
  • Log Data: IP address (anonymized), access time, error logs

C. Payment Information (Premium Users)

  • Payment transaction information is processed by Google Play Store or Apple App Store
  • We do not directly collect or store credit card information
  • Only transaction ID, subscription status, and payment date are collected

2. Purpose of Personal Information Collection and Use

2.1 Service Provision

  • Providing diary writing, saving, and management features
  • Providing AI image generation and emotion analysis services
  • User authentication and account management
  • Providing cloud backup and restore features

2.2 Service Improvement

  • Enhancing user experience and providing customized services
  • Error correction and performance optimization
  • Developing new features through usage pattern analysis
  • Statistical analysis and service quality improvement

2.3 Customer Support

  • Handling inquiries and customer support
  • Notifications and service-related announcements
  • Preventing violations of terms of service and fraudulent use

2.4 Marketing and Advertising (With Consent)

  • Providing customized advertisements (free users)
  • Event and promotion notifications
  • New feature and update announcements

3. Personal Information Retention and Use Period

3.1 General Principle

Personal information is destroyed without delay after the purpose of collection and use is achieved. However, information is retained for the specified period in the following cases:

3.2 Retention by User Request

  • Account maintenance: Until account deletion
  • Diary data: Until user deletion
  • Backup data: Until backup feature is disabled

3.3 Retention by Law

  • E-Commerce Act:
    • Contract or subscription withdrawal records: 5 years
    • Payment and goods supply records: 5 years
    • Consumer complaint or dispute resolution records: 3 years
  • Communications Secrecy Act:
    • Login records: 3 months

4. Personal Information Storage Location and Security

4.1 Storage Location

  • Local Storage: Diaries and images are primarily stored on user devices
  • Cloud Storage: When backup is enabled, encrypted storage on Google Firebase (US servers)
  • Service Providers: Google Cloud Platform (US, Asia regions)

4.2 Security Measures

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • AES-256 encryption for stored data
  • Access control and logging
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability patches
  • Double encryption for backup data

Notice: No method of online transmission or electronic storage can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. We use commercially acceptable means to protect personal information, but we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Subject Rights and Exercise Methods

Users can exercise the following privacy-related rights at any time:

7.1 View and Modify Personal Information

  • Can view and modify at any time through the settings menu in the app
  • Email address change, profile photo change, etc.

7.2 Delete Personal Information

  • Delete diaries: Individual or all diaries can be deleted
  • Delete account: Settings > Account Management > Delete Account
  • When account is deleted, all personal information is deleted immediately (except information required by law)

7.3 Data Portability

  • Settings > Data Backup > Download Data
  • Download diary content, images, and emotion analysis data in JSON format

7.4 Marketing Opt-Out

  • Can opt out of marketing notifications in Settings > Notification Settings
  • Click unsubscribe link at the bottom of emails

8. Children's Privacy Protection

ArtDiary AI is not intended for children under 14 years of age:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 14
  • If we discover that information from children under 14 has been collected, it will be deleted immediately
  • Parents or legal guardians can request deletion of children's personal information

9. Cookies and Similar Technologies

9.1 Use of Cookies

ArtDiary AI uses cookies and similar technologies for the following purposes:

  • Maintaining login status
  • Saving user settings (language, theme, etc.)
  • App usage statistics and analysis
  • Measuring ad effectiveness (free users)

9.2 Cookie Management

  • Ad tracking can be limited in device settings
  • All cookies are deleted when the app is deleted
